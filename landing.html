Features

Built for post-production professionals

Everything you need to organize, search, and navigate massive footage libraries without touching a spreadsheet.

Natural Language Search

Describe what you're looking for — "outdoor interview, blue jacket, sunset" — and CLPBTLR finds it instantly. Powered by Google Gemini.

Facial Recognition

Automatically detects and clusters faces across your entire library. Label talent once, search by name forever. Powered by InsightFace.

Audio Transcription

Every clip is transcribed with timestamps using OpenAI Whisper, running locally on your machine. Search dialogue and find the exact moment.

Professional Metadata

Resolution, FPS, codec, camera model, duration — extracted automatically via FFprobe. Filter your library by any technical spec.

How It Works

Three steps to searchable footage

1

Point it at your footage

Add watch folders — hard drives, NAS volumes, project directories. CLPBTLR monitors them and indexes new files automatically.

2

AI does the heavy lifting

Every clip is analyzed: visual content described, audio transcribed, faces detected and clustered, technical metadata extracted.

3

Search in plain English

Find any clip by describing it. Filter by camera, resolution, frame rate, date, duration, or person. Results in milliseconds.
